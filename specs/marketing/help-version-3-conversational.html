PasteCal is the calendar link that actually works—no app downloads, no "create an account first", no forwarding emails to calendar@whatever.

Just share the link and start planning. Works for team schedules, group trips, class calendars, family events—anything.

How do I share a calendar?

Pick a URL name (like pastecal.com/summer-trip) and hit save. That's your calendar.

Share the link with your team, family, or whoever. They can view and edit right away—no login required.

Pro tip: Use hard-to-guess names to keep random people out. Need a read-only link? Check the share menu.

Is this private? Can anyone find my calendar?

PasteCal doesn't list calendars anywhere—you can only access them if you know the exact URL.

Use hard-to-guess names like acme-team-q1-planning-2024 instead of obvious ones like "calendar".

Want viewers without edit access? Use the read-only link from the share menu.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yep! Open it in any browser. Or add the .ics link to your phone's calendar app:

pastecal.com/your-calendar.ics

That way you get notifications, reminders, and it syncs with your other calendars.

I have multiple calendars. How do I switch between them?

Hover over the PasteCal logo in the top-left. You'll see a dropdown of your recently viewed calendars.

Click any one to jump right to it.

Can I customize event colors and labels?

Yes! Click the settings icon (⚙️) in the top menu, then scroll to "Calendar Settings".

You can rename event types and pick custom colors. Changes apply for everyone viewing the calendar.
