How do I share a calendar?

Pick a URL like pastecal.com/summer-trip and save. That's your calendar.

Share the link—everyone can view and edit instantly. No account needed.

Privacy tip: Use hard-to-guess names. Get read-only links from the share menu.

Is this private?

Calendars aren't listed anywhere—only people with the exact URL can access them.

Use hard-to-guess names like acme-team-q1-2024 to keep random people out.

Need view-only access? Share the read-only link from the share menu.

Can I use this on my phone?

Works in any browser. Or add the .ics link to your phone's calendar app:

pastecal.com/your-calendar.ics

You'll get notifications, reminders, and it syncs with your other calendars.

How do I switch between calendars?

Hover over the PasteCal logo in the top-left to see your recently viewed calendars.
