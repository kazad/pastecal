Questions

Do the people I share with need an account?+

Never. Only you (the owner) sign in. Everyone you share with just opens the link — that's the whole point of PasteCal.

What happens to my existing calendars?+

Sign in and "claim" any calendar you already made (you just need its edit link). Nothing moves or breaks — you simply gain control over it.

Can I cancel?+

Anytime, in one click. Your calendars stay; they just revert to the free, open-edit mode.

How is this different from Google Calendar?+

Google Calendar needs everyone to have a Google account and fiddle with permissions. PasteCal is a single link anyone can open in a browser — with optional Pro control when you need it.
